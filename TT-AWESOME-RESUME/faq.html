Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a resume?

To get started, simply visit our website and click on the "create resume" button. Use the templets to input your personal information, education, work experience, skills, and any additional sections you'd like to include in your resume.

Can I customize the design and layout of my resume?

Yes, you can choose from a variety of professionally designed templates and customize it. Our intuitive editor allows you to make changes easily.

What should I include in my resume/CV for academic positions?

For academic positions, include your educational background, research experience, publications, conference presentations, teaching experience, and any relevant certifications. Tailor it to highlight your academic achievements.

Can I download my resume as a PDF file?

Yes, once you've completed your resume, you can download it as a PDF file. This ensures that it maintains its formatting and can be easily shared with employers.

Can I download multiple resumes ?

Yes, you can save multiple versions of your resume with different content or styles. This feature allows you to tailor your resume for different job applications.

Do you provide any guidance on writing resume summaries or objectives?

Yes, you can chat with our AI mentor "LISA" to get summary or objective and aslo clarify any of your doubts.

How do I showcase my skills on my resume?

Include a dedicated section for skills on your resume. List both hard skills (e.g., programming languages) and soft skills (e.g., communication, teamwork). Be sure to align them with the job requirements.
